Top Ramen

Description:

A classic soup to feed your soul. This dish comes in many flavors. My personal suggestion is Chicken.

Pro-Tip:

Doctoring up the broth with some cabinet herbs is a nice change of pace.

Ingredients:

  • 6 - Cups of Water
  • 1 - Package of Top Ramen

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add Ramen noodles to water.
  3. Cook for 3 mins.
  4. Strain water into a bowl for the broth.
  5. Add flavor packet to the water and stir in noodles.
  6. Let cool for 2 minutes and begin slurping.
